Saiprakash Kuckian

Vice President, Appco Group

Saiprakash’s journey in Appco India began in late 1998 when he came across an advert in the newspaper for a UK-based firm recruiting for a sales professional.

Sai’s experience and expertise has been instrumental in shaping up the DTH (direct to home), Charities and Health card divisions in India. Under the strong leadership of Vice President Tony Fernandez, Sai admits to having a challenging yet exciting time building up the business in the nascent Indian market.

His efforts and strong work successes led to him being promoted to Organisational Head in 2004 and in 2008, he was the first Asian in the company to be promoted to Vice President of the Appco Group.

Over the last decade, Sai has developed a strong team of Organisational Heads and top Owners. He has developed a successful organisation consistently setting a fast pace in the industry.
