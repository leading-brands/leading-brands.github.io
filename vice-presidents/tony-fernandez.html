Tony Fernandez

Vice President, Appco Group India, Country Head, India and USA

With more than 25 years’ experience, Tony Fernandez, is truly an epitome of success, inspiration and leadership. He has been instrumental in managing the operations in India and leading it to success.

Tony started his career with Appco in Perth, Australia and within four months, he went on to become the top performer in his division and progressed to Ownership. His demonstration of skillful expertise, business management, operational efficiency and result-oriented approach made him one of the most notable personalities in Appco Group.

In 1998, Tony was given the opportunity to open up an office in India where he has created a flourishing business. Appco India currently has a presence in more than 24 major cities.

Following on from the success in India, Tony has also expanded his network and set up operations in Japan and South Korea. Tony has also taken on a much bigger challenge of developing the US business and is involved in setting up a number of divisions and creating a positive Appco identity in the challenging US markets.

His vast experiences across a number of geographies have proven his abilities as a proficient leader in India and abroad.
