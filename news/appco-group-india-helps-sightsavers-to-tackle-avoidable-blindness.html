Appco Group India helps Sightsavers to tackle avoidable blindness

Appco Group India has entered into a partnership with leading global development organisation Sightsavers. The objective of the partnership is to help eradicate avoidable blindness by speaking to potential donors and spreading the message across the country. 

Fundraisers working with Appco Group India have signed up more than 2,000 donors in just six months, which is a major accomplishment both for Sightsavers and for Appco.

Acknowledging Appco’s contribution, a leading spokesperson from Sightsavers said the charity is impressed with Appco’s accomplishment to date and is looking forward to continuing a healthy and prosperous relationship with the company.

Sightsavers has been working in India since 1966 to eliminate avoidable blindness and to ensure that people who are irreversibly blind are adequately supported to lead lives of independence and dignity.
