Appco Group India expands its reach to Tier 2 & Tier 3 towns in India

Appco Group India has reached to potential donors & customers not just in metro cities, but has also expanded its promotional campaigns into Tier 2 & Tier 3 towns, giving clients an increasing donor & customer base.

Appco Group India & its marketing companies currently operate in 19 cities across the country but are also tapping potential towns & cities through road trip promotions for its charity & non commercial clients. Road trips are a unique way of promotions where Appco sales professionals analyse potential territories & execute planned campaigns for reaching potential customers & donors.

In 2015 alone, Appco professionals have successfully undertaken road trips to 120+ towns across the country for charity & commercial clients, generating huge sales volumes through these promotions.

The huge success of road trip promotions has garnered positive feedback from the clients too. Currently, there are more 30+ towns which will be rolled out for different client promotions within the group.
