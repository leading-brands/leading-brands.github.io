Appco India breaks all records for major PayTV client Tata Sky

Appco Group India has broken records yet again with over 4100 activations in just one month for PayTV client Tata Sky.

In the last three months, the team has expanded to an all time high of 250 Field Representatives, who have delivered impressive numbers for the client with their commitment and dedication.

The Client Tata Sky is delighted with the independent marketing companies associated with Appco Group India for signing up more than 4000 activations in July & August each.

The client quoted- “The last three months from July to September has been extremely fruitful, with APPCO consistently clocking in 4000+ activations each month. The APPCO sales team has raised the level of performance with some cities like Hyderabad, Vizag, Kolkata and Jaipur deserving special mention for their performances. Thank you for all the efforts you have put in to reach this milestone.”

 Appco India’s partnership with Tata Sky goes back to 2012 and it has helped thousands of households switch from cable TV to Pay TV.
