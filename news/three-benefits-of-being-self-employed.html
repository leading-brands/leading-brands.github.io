Three benefits of being self-employed

Self-employment is a great option if you want more control over your working hours and career progression. It’s also great if you’re looking to factor your work in around other commitment, such as study or family.

Of course, self-employment is not for everyone, but it is certainly an option that has worked for many independent contractors within the Appco India network.

If you are considering a self-employed role – or you’re in one already – take a look at the recent Appco UK blog, which highlights three key benefits associated with working for yourself.
