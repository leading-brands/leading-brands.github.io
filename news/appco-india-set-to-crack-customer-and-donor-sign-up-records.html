Appco India set to crack customer and donor sign-up records

Appco India has come into 2015 swinging, boosting its monthly sign-ups for charity and commercial clients by more than 10% and nearing a target of 100,000 customer and donor signed for its clients.

Taking inspiration from a successful 2014, Appco Group India is pursuing new avenues of growth this year, and has welcomed new clients in both its healthcare and charity divisions.

India has also made great strides in raising funds and promoting innovative campaigns for commercial clients – across insurance, pay TV and health care services – and non-profit partners, including in areas of child empowerment, elderly care, education, disaster management.

Appco Group India Vice President Tony Fernandez attributes this success to numerous strategic initiatives, including “newer methods of fundraising, increased recruitment, potential city launches, improved sales training methods and operational efficiency”.
