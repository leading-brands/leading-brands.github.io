Appco India provides emergency aid to thousands of Kashmir flood victims

Appco Group India’s Charity division has helped secure more than US$45,000 in donations in a month from 10 September 2014 for the flood victims in Jammu and Kashmir.

Face-to-face fundraisers working with Appco Group India and Save The Children have signed up over 800 donors while raising awareness of the cause in Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Coimbatore and Hyderabad.

The donations raised will help Save The Children provide emergency relief for the victims of the Jammu and Kashmir floods, which devastated the region, claiming more than 150 lives.
