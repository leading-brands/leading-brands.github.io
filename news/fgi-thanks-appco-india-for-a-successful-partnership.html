FGI thanks Appco India for a successful partnership

Future Generali Insurance has presented a memento of thanks & appreciation to the FRs contracted with Appco for helping them in delivering business objectives in the year 2014-15.

Marketing companies associated with Appco India have been delivering 5000+ units of insurance sales each month since the start of 2015. Appco India has also helped the client to reach into 11 cities while also increasing promotions in 40+ towns through road trips.

Currently, more than 200+ contracted FRs have been promoting shopkeepers insurance & accidental insurance products to increase brand awareness for FGI, thereby helping them to penetrate into tough markets. FRs contracted with Appco have also been working hard to bring up the quality percentages with the client. 

Appco India has been associated with FGI since 2010 & this partnership has helped FGI scale new heights.
