Heart Research Institute calls facility ‘the house that Appco built’

The Heart Research Institute (HRI) in Sydney has shown its appreciation for Appco Group by dubbing its facility “the house that Appco built”.

The nickname was revealed when a group of fundraisers, who are generating support for the HRI in New Zealand, had the chance to visit the Australian medical research centre.

Senior international fundraising executive Sharon Butt was pleased to meet some of the people whose day-to-day roles are to increase awareness and funding for the organisation.

“We had such a great day with the team from New Zealand. They all talked about how much they love what they do and how committed they are – it was very inspiring,” she said.
