‘Appco is a world of opportunities’, says Appco contracted business Owner

Growing up in a metro city like Mumbai, Nadeem Khan (pictured above) always had the passion for business. Currently an independent business Owner contracted with Appco Group India, Nadeem is all praises about the many opportunities that he experienced through an association with Appco.

Nadeem, who is responsible for the successfully setting up his marketing company in Jaipur, had the opportunity to travel to Appco Group’s Korea & Japan offices for a 1-week long trip & learn the new practices that people in our network are following.

Nadeem feels that such exposure opportunities have helped him understand & learn how to conduct his business better. He said, “Such opportunities are a great help to independent business owners because you not only understand how systems in our business works around the world but you also get to meet new people, share their experiences and learn a lot of new practices which can be implemented in your offices and can be passed on to the new professionals in the network. Also, you get to know how vast Appco’s business around the world is.”

Nadeem also had the opportunity to independently expand the Appco business in the new city, Jaipur. Appco’s development model helps business Owners contracted with the Group to take advantage of the opportunities be it exposure trips, business freedom, self employment, operational support & many such advantages to help them conduct their business better.
