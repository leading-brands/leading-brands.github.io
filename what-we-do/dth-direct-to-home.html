DTH (direct to home)

Appco India’s DTH (direct to home) division acquires quality customers each year. We are committed to promoting DTH products to an ever-expanding market with our unique customer centric promotions.

Working with major DTH service providers including Tata Sky, the division has already helped more 300,000 households switch from cable to DTH since 2012. Our team of professionals have helped this division continue to grow with an increase in sales every month.

Expansion into new cities is already underway. We are now present in major Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities in India, including Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Coimbatore, Chennai and others.

Visit our Contact us page to get in touch with one of our DTH specialists.
