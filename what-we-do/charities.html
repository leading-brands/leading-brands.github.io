Charities

Appco Group Support is a specialised division and is the largest face-to-face donor recruitment agency in the world. Through our direct marketing campaigns, our network is continually working to provide the much-needed funds and resources for our charity partners.

Since launching in India in 2001, our team of dedicated fundraisers has helped to enroll quality and committed donors by raising awareness of the work done by our clients to more than a million people across the country annually.

Working with a number of leading charity organisations in India, we continually set and maintain the highest standards of ethics in the fundraising community. We are committed to meeting the highest standards of quality, integrity and accountability at all times with donors and the charities we represent to create more awareness for the causes we support.

Visit our Contact Us page to get in touch with one of our Charity specialists.
