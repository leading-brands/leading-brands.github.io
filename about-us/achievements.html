Achievements

We are enormously proud of our achievements and the value we add, not only to the global business community, but also to the global community at large. Here is a snapshot of some of our key achievements… 

  • We have secured millions in donations for our fundraising clients between 1998 and 2014.
  • We have converted more than 325,000 new customers from cable TV to DTH (direct to home) on behalf of our entertainment clients.
  • We have successfully insured the lives of thousands of customers in India between 2008 and 2015 on behalf of our insurance client.
  • We have acquired more than 20,000 customers for our credit card partner since launching in late 2013.
  • Appco Group India works across 4 major industry sectors and has in excess of 12 major clients across the country.
